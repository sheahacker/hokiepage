These are my classes this semester.

I'm taking 5 classes this semester. They are all for my majors, Creative Writing and Professional and Technical Writing. They are Read and Write English Studies, Creative Writing: Fiction, Intro to World Literature, American Literary History and Writing and Digital Media.

Read and Write English Studies

We are currently reading a novel called Storming Heaven and using it to analyze how it is written and how we can become better writings by studying it.

Creative Writing: Fiction

Taught by one of the best professors I've ever had, in this class we write fiction pieces and work together as a class to edit them and make them the best they can be.

Intro to World Literature

In this online class, we study the historical and aethestic context of many different works of literature from around the world.

American Literary History

We study literary works that date back as early to the Mayflower and the Massachusetts Bay Colony, to works as recent as ones we ready today.

Writing and Digital Media

In this class we have been learning how to work with HTML, and are now beginning to work with Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) to work on websites.
